A coffee is a great way to pick yourself up in the morning, but remember that those fancy drinks will add up over time. A great way to save money is by making your own coffee at home. However, everyone deserves to treat themselves! If you have money available in your budget, plan for a coffee that can be bought for special occasions or when you are in too much of a hurry at home.
