At least 20% of your income should go to savings. Saving money is a necessity, even though it might not feel like it, because you will get old and want to retire. Also, life is unpredictable, and you never know what might happen in the future. Whether it is a future concert or trip, or something dire like a medical emergency, it is always best to be more prepared than less.
