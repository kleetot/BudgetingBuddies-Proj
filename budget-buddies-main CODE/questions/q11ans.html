Whether it is for school or work, you have to get there somehow. Transportation will always be a factor in your life, but there are often ways to mitigate the cost. Investing in a bike is a good way to save money if you live too far to walk, but want to save money on gas. If you utilize public transportation, applying for a pass is often less expensive than repeatedly paying.
