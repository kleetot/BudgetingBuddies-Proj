FAQ


How does it work?

Click on “Create Your Budget” on the Home Page. This will direct you to questions for you to answer about your current spending habits. Each question will give you spending feedback based on your responses!

What if i don’t know exactly how much I spend?

Our answer choices for each question will give you a price range, rather than requiring you to type in an exact price. If you aren’t sure exactly how much you spend, try to estimate! In the future, keeping better track of how much you spend on different things will allow you to overall be better at budgeting.

How can I keep track of how much I spend?

There are many ways to keep track of your spending habits. You could use a spreadsheet, a notebook, choose out of several apps, etc. There is no wrong way to keep track of it and it depends on your preferences. Choose whatever is easier!

How should I make a budget?

Decide what is important to you and what you want to prioritize. If there is something you want to save money for, set goals! Keep your income in mind and then plan how much money you will spend each month. Make sure to include all of your monthly expenses!
